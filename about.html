My Education

Education is the foundation of my journey. Here are some key phases of my learning experience:

Primary School

My journey began in primary school.I had the privilege of attending Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Selasih (SK Taman Selasih) for my primary school education. Located in a warm and welcoming neighborhood, SK Taman Selasih provided the foundation for my academic journey and personal growth. The school is well-known for its commitment to nurturing young minds through quality education and a supportive environment.

Secondary School

After completing my primary education, I continued my academic journey at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Taman Selasih (SMK Taman Selasih). This transition marked an important phase in my life as I stepped into a more challenging and dynamic learning environment. SMK Taman Selasih is renowned for its dedication to academic excellence and co-curricular development, and it truly lived up to its reputation during my time there..

Diploma

After completing secondary school, I took a significant step toward higher education by enrolling in Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kedah to pursue a diploma. UiTM Kedah, located in the serene and scenic town of Merbok, became my home for academic and personal growth during this chapter of my life..
